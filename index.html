Project Overview

Geospatial analysis of flood vulnerability zones overlaid with urban green infrastructure. Designed to generate labeled training data for AI flood-risk prediction models. Methodology: QGIS 3.34, DEM-derived slope analysis, NDVI from Sentinel-2, and census-weighted risk scoring.

Methodology

1. DEM acquisition (USGS 1m LiDAR)
2. Slope + flow accumulation via GRASS r.watershed
3. NDVI from Sentinel-2 Band 4/8 (QGIS Raster Calculator)
4. Risk scoring: weighted overlay (slope 40%, NDVI 35%, imperviousness 25%)
5. Polygon vectorization → attribute labeling for AI training
6. Validation against FEMA flood maps (F1=0.84)
